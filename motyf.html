Moving Type Festival is an International gathering of typographic exemplars. The International Symposium and Media Art Exhibition showcases the current and future forms of interactive and motion typography expressed through, art design and technology.
In Branding this festival, I wanted my approach to focus on the expansion and growth of typography, as an never ending growing element within design. By creating a new typeface, I wanted that to influence the idea of a future brand. With a repetative visual style I wanted to give a sense of never ending growth within typography.
