Moving Type Festival is an International gathering of typographic exemplars - a collision of minds. The International Symposium and Media Art Exhibition showcases the current and future forms of interactive
and motion typography expressed through
art, design, space and technology.
In Branding this festival, I wanted my approach to focus on the expansion and growth of typography, as an never ending growing element of design. By creating a new typeface I wanted that to influence the idea of a future brand. With a repetative visual style I wanted to give a snese of growth within typography.
