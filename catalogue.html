For this project I had to propose a catalogue showcasing the architectual work by Le Corbusier. The catalogue was a means of advertisement for the upcoming exhibition illustrating Corbusiers designs. It was important to approach this in a reductive and minimalistic style in order to articulate Corbusiers aesthetic.
Titled Light, Space, Air, I wanted to imitate Corbusiers design principles throughout the catalogue. Emulating lots of space, a geometric method, subtle colour with a reductive approach, I wanted to articulate Le Corbusiers clean and minimal style.
