Branding & Identity Typography
Motyf is a moving type design festival for people who use type in art, design and space. The event focuses on the expansion and growth of typography within a virtual environment. Devoted to contemporary typography, with talks, workshops and experiences focusing on where typography is today and where it might lead to in the future.
I wanted my identity to reflect the growth and expansion of typography by creating overlapping growing images to give a sense that type is expanding while maintaining a virtual and contemporary appearance.
