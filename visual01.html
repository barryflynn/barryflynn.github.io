Visual 01 is a conceptual project that seeks to imagine what the future for cybersecurity looks like. With the ever-escalating risk to our digital identities and the increase in recent data breaches and cyber attacks, developing a more secure approach to authentication is a major priority across current business and secuirty landscapes. Visual 01 is a near future product designed to tackle this problem.
With the latest authentication method combining visual interpretation with involuntary memory, the worlds first automatic and unconscious password formula is introduced.
