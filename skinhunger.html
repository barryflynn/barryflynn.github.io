Skin Hunger
Skin Hunger
Motion Graphics Film Titles
Skin Hunger is an experimental film that deals with the emotive and biological need for human touch. The visual style of Skin Hunger is a mix of natural and the abstract, an immersive exploration into the organ that is our skin.
With a focus on texture, space and movement, the film visualises the tension around the need and the want for skin contact. I wanted to create something visually strong while maintaining an element of absence.
