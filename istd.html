Children of war is a book published dedicated to the unfortunate demise of the children taken by the Refugee Crisis. It focuses on the Syrian War, the Meditarranean crisis, International Involvement and the children effected. It aims to acknowledge and recognise the severity of the worlds biggest and most tragic crisis to date.
By approaching this project in a humble manner I chose a more subtle direction to emphasize a more serious tone of the subect matter. Throughout the book, it quietly highlights a list of Syrian names that represent the over 7000 children who have died due to this crisis.
